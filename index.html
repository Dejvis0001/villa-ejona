About Us

Emiljano Apartment, nestled in Sarandë, offers air-conditioned rooms, private check-in/out, and complimentary WiFi. With a cozy bedroom, well-equipped kitchen, and balcony with city views, guests can unwind in comfort. Explore nearby attractions like Sarande Main Beach and Butrint National Park. Corfu International Airport is conveniently located 35 km away.
