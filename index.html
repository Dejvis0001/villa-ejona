Villa EJONA
Welcome to EJONA Villa, ideally situated just 400 meters from Durres Beach in Durrës. Our modern apartment complex offers comfortable rooms with air conditioning, free private parking, and complimentary WiFi.
Each unit features a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom for your convenience. Explore nearby attractions like the Rock of Kavaje and the Durres Amphitheatre, or venture further to Skanderbeg Square and the Dajti Ekspres Cable Car. With easy access to Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport just 32 km away, EJONA Villa is your perfect base for discovering the beauty of Durrës. We look forward to hosting you!
