About Us

Be more than satisfied with us!

Unifying individuals across the globe.

Set in Durrës, 600 metres from Durres Main Beach and 1 km from Saranda City Beach, Villa Ejona offers air conditioning. Among the facilities at this property are private check-in and check-out and bicycle parking, along with free WiFi throughout the property. La Petite Beach is 1.9 km from the apartment and Butrint National Park is 17 km away.

The apartment comes with 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a balcony with city views. Guests can take in the ambience of the surroundings from an outdoor dining area. For added privacy, the accommodation has a private entrance and soundproofing. The nearest airport is Corfu International Airport, 35 km from the apartment.
